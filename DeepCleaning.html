    Beyond the Essentials—It's Deep Clean Time

    Standard cleaning is great for keeping up, but sometimes your home needs a little extra love—the kind that scrubs, polishes, and revives every inch. That's where Deep Cleaning comes in.

    Think of it as a glow-up for your home. We go beyond the basics, tackling the details that often get overlooked—baseboards, door frames, vent returns, switch plates, and even those hard-to-reach nooks that have been quietly collecting dust.

    And of course, everything included in our Standard Cleaning is covered: dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom sanitization, and more. We just take it up a notch with extra attention to detail.

    Whether you're prepping for guests, refreshing your space, or just craving that "just-moved-in" level of clean, our deep cleaning service is the ultimate reset.

    ✨ More shine. More sparkle. More 'ahhh, that's better.'

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Deep cleaning isn't just a more intense standard cleaning—it's the next level of fresh. It's for the details, the overlooked, the "I'll get to it later" spots that, let's be real, no one ever actually gets to.

    At iQ Home Care, we take your home beyond the surface to give it a true top-to-bottom refresh.

    • More Than Surface-Level Clean – We don't just dust—most deep-cleaned areas get a wet-wash treatment to eliminate grime, buildup, and lingering fingerprints.
    • The Little Details, Handled – We focus on the often-overlooked spots that collect dust and dirt, from baseboards to vent returns and everything in between.
    • Customized to Your Home – We tailor the deep clean to your home's needs—whether it's refreshing high-traffic areas or tackling long-neglected corners.

    ✨ Deep cleaning is the difference between "looks clean" and "actually clean." And once you experience it, you'll never go back.

    Our 4-Step Deep Cleaning Process
    • Step 1: Book & Customize - Scheduling is easy! Book online in just a few clicks, select any add-ons, and tailor the service to your home's needs. Prefer to talk it through first? Our friendly team is happy to help—just give us a call!
    • Step 2: Arrival & Home Assessment - Our team arrives ready to work, reviewing the areas that need extra attention and ensuring we meet your deep cleaning goals.
    • Step 3: The Deep Clean Transformation - We tackle the details standard cleaning doesn't cover—wet-washing (most) baseboards, doorframes, switch plates, vent returns, and accessible lighting fixtures—while giving every room a comprehensive refresh.
    • Step 4: Come Home to the Cleanest Clean - Your home is now cleaner, fresher, and more polished than ever. All that's left for you to do? Kick back and enjoy it.

    ✨ Deep cleaning isn't just about tidying up—it's a whole new level of fresh.
