    House Washing

    What Is House Washing & Why Does It Matter?

    Over time, your home's exterior collects dirt, mold, algae, and other contaminants that dull its appearance and, if left unchecked, can cause long-term damage. House washing is a safe, effective way to restore your home's exterior and protect it from the elements.

    At iQ Home Care, we use soft washing—a low-pressure cleaning method combined with professional-grade solutions—to break down grime without damaging delicate surfaces. Unlike high-pressure washing, which can be too harsh for siding, stucco, and painted surfaces, soft washing is safe, thorough, and long-lasting.

    If your home hasn't been cleaned in a while, don't worry—you're not alone! Many homeowners don't realize how much buildup accumulates over time. Our first-time deep clean washes away years of dirt, instantly refreshing your home's curb appeal and setting the foundation for long-term protection.

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Whether it's your first house wash or you're keeping up with routine maintenance, our process is simple, effective, and tailored to your home's unique needs.

    With iQ Home Care, you'll receive a full-coverage service. Our house washing service includes siding, soffits, fascias, and the gutter exterior—giving your entire facade a fresh, uniform clean.

    Our house washing process includes:

    • Step 1: Assessment - Every home is different. We evaluate your property to determine the best cleaning approach.
    • Step 2: Protection - We take extra care to safeguard landscaping, furniture, and delicate surfaces before starting the wash.
    • Step 3: Soft Wash Cleaning - Our low-pressure wash and professional-grade cleaning solution remove dirt, algae, and buildup without damage—safe for all surfaces.
    • Step 4: Final Inspection - We ensure an even, spotless clean and walk you through the results to guarantee your satisfaction.

    Keeping Your Home Looking Its Best

    Once your home is fresh and clean, we help you protect that investment. Homes surrounded by trees, lakes, or high humidity may need more frequent care, while others can go longer between washes. If you're unsure how often your home needs cleaning, we'll provide honest recommendations based on your home's environment—no unnecessary services, just what makes sense for you.

    • Enhanced Curb Appeal
    • Protects Surface Integrity
    • Prevents Premature Aging
    • Removes Harmful Contaminants
    • Increases Property Value

    A house wash isn't just about aesthetics—it's about preserving your home, preventing costly damage, and maintaining a space you're proud of.
