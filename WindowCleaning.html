    Why Professional Window Cleaning Makes a Difference

    Over time, windows accumulate dust, pollen, hard water stains, and streaks that dull their appearance and obstruct your view. Many homeowners don't realize how much buildup occurs—or how professional window cleaning can instantly restore clarity and brightness to their home.

    At iQ Home Care, we provide streak-free, professional-grade cleaning for both interior and exterior windows. Our expert team removes grime without harsh chemicals or damage, so your windows look spotless and let in more natural light.

    If your windows haven't been cleaned in a while, don't worry—you're not alone! Our first-time deep clean eliminates built-up grime, instantly refreshing your home's appearance and setting the foundation for effortless upkeep.

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Whether it's your first professional window cleaning or part of your regular home care routine, our process is simple, effective, and tailored to your home's unique needs.

    Full-Service Exterior Cleaning Includes: Cleaning screens, panes, frames, and sill —ensuring your windows are crystal clear, streak-free, and looking their absolute best.

    Interior Window Cleaning Available: We also offer interior window cleaning, including tracks, panes, frames, and sills —for homeowners who want a completely spotless home, inside and out. (More details below!)

    Our window cleaning process includes:
    • Step 1: Assessment - We inspect your windows to identify hard water stains, buildup, and screen condition to determine the best approach for a flawless finish.
    • Step 2: Protection - We take extra care to safeguard surrounding areas, including landscaping, furniture, and delicate window frames.
    • Step 3: Streak-Free Cleaning – Powered by Advanced Filtration - Unlike traditional methods, we use a cutting-edge water filtration system that removes minerals, impurities, and residue from the water—ensuring a pure, streak-free clean that dries crystal clear.
    • Step 4: Final Inspection - We carefully check for streaks, smudges, or missed spots to guarantee a spotless finish.

    Keeping Your Windows Looking Their Best

    Once your windows are crystal clear, we help you maintain that streak-free shine with recommendations tailored to your home.

    Interior Window Cleaning for a Complete Refresh

    Your home's interior windows collect dust, smudges, and buildup just like the exterior—but they often go unnoticed. Our interior window cleaning service removes fingerprints, streaks, and debris, leaving your home brighter and clearer inside and out.

    • Tracks, panes, frames, and sills cleaned with care

    • Safe, streak-free cleaning with professional tools

    • Perfect for seasonal deep cleaning or routine upkeep
