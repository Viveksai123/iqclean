    Brilliant Lighting, Effortless Control

    Outdoor lighting should be as dynamic as your life. Whether you're illuminating your home for everyday elegance, setting the mood for a backyard gathering, or celebrating a special occasion, permanent lighting puts complete control at your fingertips.

    With fully customizable colors, endless animation effects, and smart app control, you can instantly switch from warm, ambient lighting to vibrant holiday displays. Whether it's a soft glow for everyday curb appeal or a dazzling show for a big event, your home always shines exactly the way you want.

    Our professionally installed low-profile lighting blends seamlessly into your home's exterior—nearly invisible by day, stunning by night. Designed for year-round performance, weather resistance, and energy efficiency, it's lighting that adapts to every season, every mood, and every moment.

    ✨ Unmatched Customization. Ultimate Convenience. With permanent lighting, the possibilities are endless.

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Permanent lighting isn't just about aesthetics—it's about seamless integration, smart technology, and lasting durability. Our system is designed to provide brilliant, customizable lighting while blending discreetly into your home's architecture.

    What's Included?

    • Custom Track Design & Color Matching – Our track is customized to blend seamlessly with your home's trim and fascia. With multiple track profiles available, we ensure a perfect fit for any architectural style.
    • Low-Profile, Architectural Design – The fixtures are discreetly installed under eaves and soffits, keeping the system virtually invisible during the day.
    • Smart Hub & Intuitive App Control – Change colors, set schedules, and create custom lighting effects from your phone or tablet.
    • 4-Diode LED System – Each light features RGB-WW technology (Red, Green, Blue, and Dedicated Warm White LEDs), delivering true warm whites and vibrant colors with smooth dimming capabilities.
    • Weatherproof & Built to Last – Designed for year-round exposure, the system is water-resistant, UV-protected, and built to withstand harsh conditions.
    • Zoning & Animation Effects – Create unique lighting zones, customize movement patterns, and set holiday presets for effortless celebrations.

    The result? Stunning, energy-efficient lighting that's as versatile as it is durable—without the hassle of hanging lights ever again.

    Our 5-Step Installation Process
    • Step 1: Consultation & Customization - We start with a free, personalized consultation to understand your vision and needs. Whether you want subtle architectural lighting or bold, dynamic displays, we help design the perfect system for your home.
    • Step 2: Professional Planning & Layout - Using precise measurements and careful planning, we map out the ideal placement for seamless integration. Our team ensures the lighting follows the natural architecture of your home for a flawless, low-profile installation.
    • Step 3: Expert Installation - Our technicians install the durable, weather-resistant track system along soffits, fascia, or rooflines. Each fixture is secured with no exposed wires or bulky hardware, keeping your home's exterior sleek and polished.
    • Step 4: System Integration & Testing - Once installed, we connect your system to the smart hub and mobile app, ensuring everything is properly synced. We walk you through how to create custom lighting scenes, schedules, and effects, so you can make the most of your new system.
    • Step 5: Final Walkthrough & Client Support - We complete a full system check and provide hands-on guidance so you feel confident controlling your lights. Plus, our team is always available for support should you need adjustments or have questions down the road.

    Built to Last – Longevity & Warranty

    Investing in permanent lighting means years of beauty, convenience, and reliability. Our system is engineered for long-term durability while offering an easy, low-maintenance experience.

    Exceptional Lifespan & Performance

    • Built to Last for Decades—our premium LED technology ensures long-lasting brilliance and reliability.
    • Weatherproof & UV-Protected – Designed to withstand rain, snow, heat, and humidity without fading or failing.
    • Seamless, Low-Profile Design – No bulky wires, no seasonal hassle—just beautiful, maintenance-free lighting.
    • A Smart Investment – Unlike temporary décor or seasonal lighting, permanent lighting enhances curb appeal and increases property value, making it a smart, lasting home improvement.

    Industry-Leading Warranty & Ongoing Support

    Your lighting system is backed by an industry-leading 30-year warranty—20 years through Gemstone Lighting plus an additional 10 years covered by iQ Home Care.

    • Transferable Warranty – If you sell your home, the coverage stays with the property—boosting its long-term value.
    • Ongoing Support – Whether you need troubleshooting, adjustments, or upgrades, we're here to ensure you love your lights for years to come.
