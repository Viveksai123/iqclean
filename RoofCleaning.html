    The Secret to a Longer-Lasting Roof? Routine Cleaning.

    Over time, shingle, tile, metal, and slate roofs accumulate debris, black streaks, moss, lichen, and algae that not only make your home look worn down but can also shorten the lifespan of your roofing materials. These debris and organic growths trap moisture, leading to premature aging, structural weakening, and costly repairs if left untreated.

    At iQ Home Care, we specialize in soft-wash roof cleaning—a safe, non-damaging method that effectively removes stains, algae, and debris while preserving your roof's integrity. Whether you have asphalt shingles, clay or concrete tiles, metal panels, or slate roofing, professional roof cleaning extends the life of your roof, enhances curb appeal, and helps maintain a healthier home exterior.

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Unlike pressure washing, which can strip protective granules from shingles, etch delicate tiles, or corrode metal surfaces, our soft-wash system is designed to thoroughly clean without damage.

    We use a specialized soft-wash treatment that safely lifts and removes black streaks, moss, lichen, and algae while also rinsing gutters and downspouts to prevent future buildup. This process restores your roof's color, structural integrity, and longevity—all while keeping your home's exterior in top condition.

    Our 4-Step Roof Cleaning Process:
    • Step 1: Roof Inspection - We assess staining, moss growth, roofing material, and surrounding areas to determine the safest and most effective cleaning approach. We also take note of delicate landscaping and exterior surfaces that require extra protection.
    • Step 2: Surface Protection - Before cleaning begins, we rinse and shield delicate plants and any at-risk areas to prevent runoff exposure.
    • Step 3: Soft Wash Cleaning & Drainage Check - Using our gentle yet powerful soft-wash system and cleaning agents, we safely remove black streaks, moss, and algae, restoring your roof's original color and integrity without high pressure. As part of the process, we provide a thorough final rinse of all treated areas, flush downspouts, and clear debris to ensure proper water drainage from the roof.
    • Step 4: Final Check & Preventative Tips - Once cleaned, we inspect for any remaining buildup and provide tips on preventing future staining, helping you keep your roof in great shape for years to come.

    Keeping Your Roof Looking Its Best

    Routine maintenance is key to preventing costly repairs and keeping your roof in top condition.

    Annual or Bi-Annual Cleanings help prevent moss and algae from taking hold. Trimming Overhanging Branches reduces shade and excess moisture, limiting organic growth. Ensuring Proper Drainage prevents water buildup that can contribute to roof stains.

    Keep Your Roof Clean. Keep Your Home Protected.

    Your roof protects your home—give it the care it deserves. iQ Home Care's soft wash roof cleaning removes stains, moss, and algae safely, restoring your roof's beauty and longevity. Extend its lifespan, boost curb appeal, and prevent costly repairs with professional roof cleaning.
