    The Essentials—Handled.

    Keeping up with house cleaning can feel like a full-time job—but it doesn't have to be. Whether you're balancing work, family, or just want to spend less time scrubbing and more time enjoying life, our Standard Cleaning service makes maintaining a fresh, tidy home effortless.

    Routine cleaning made simple—so you can focus on what truly matters.

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Keeping your home fresh and tidy isn't just about what we clean—it's about how we clean. Our team focuses on high-touch areas, everyday buildup, and the details that matter most, ensuring your home is left feeling clean, comfortable, and refreshed.

    What's Included?

    • Kitchen & Bathrooms – Wipe down surfaces, clean sinks, sanitize countertops, and spot-clean cabinet exteriors. Interior microwave cleaning is also included for a fresh and tidy finish.
    • Dusting & Surface Cleaning – Dust furniture, decor, and reachable shelves to reduce allergens and maintain a tidy home.
    • Vacuuming & Mopping – Floors throughout the home are vacuumed, and hard floors are mopped for a fresh, clean feel.
    • Trash Removal – All wastebaskets are emptied, leaving your home neat and refreshed.
    • Bedroom & Living Spaces – Tidy up common areas, straighten pillows and blankets, make beds, and even change sheets (upon request).

    Need a deeper clean? Upgrade to our Deep Cleaning service for a top-to-bottom refresh, including millwork, door frames, switch plates, lighting fixtures, and more! Learn More Here

    Our 4-Step Standard Cleaning Process
    • Step 1: Book & Customize - Schedule your cleaning online and tailor your service—exclude areas, add requests, and make it your own.
    • Step 2: Arrival & Home Assessment - Our team arrives ready to clean, assessing your home's needs before getting started.
    • Step 3: Thorough Cleaning - We follow our standard cleaning checklist, ensuring every space is fresh and tidy.
    • Step 4: Enjoy Your Fresh, Clean Home! - With everything spotless, all that's left to do is sit back, relax, and enjoy your space.

    Want to Elevate Your Clean? Add-Ons & Extras

    Your home is unique, and so is your cleaning plan. While our Standard Cleaning covers all the essentials, we offer extra services to take your clean to the next level.

    • Inside Appliances – Need your oven or fridge deep-cleaned? We've got it covered.
    • Interior Windows – Let the light shine through with spotless, streak-free windows.
    • Laundry & Bed-Making – Fresh linens and folded laundry? Yes, please!
    • And More! – Have a special request? Just ask, and we'll make it happen.

    Designed for Convenience – Quality & Reliability

    Keeping your home clean should be easy—and with iQ Home Care, it is.

    • Flexible Scheduling – Weekly, bi-weekly, triweekly, monthly, or every 6 weeks—whatever fits your routine best.
    • Trained & Trusted Professionals – Background-checked, detail-oriented cleaners who care.
    • Fully Equipped & Ready to Clean – Our team brings everything needed to leave your home spotless, top to bottom. Prefer we use your supplies? Just let us know!
    • 100% Satisfaction Guarantee – If you're not happy, we'll make it right.
