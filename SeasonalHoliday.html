    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Holiday lights should bring joy—not stress. From design to takedown, we handle every detail so you can enjoy a beautifully lit home without the hassle. Whether you want a warm, elegant glow or a bold, colorful display, we make holiday lighting effortless and spectacular.

    What's Included?

    • Custom Design & Consultation – We create a tailored lighting plan to match your home and holiday style.
    • Professional Installation – No ladders, no tangles—we handle everything, safely and efficiently.
    • High-Quality LED Lights – Energy-efficient, vibrant, and built to last.
    • Programmable Timer Control – Set your lights to turn on at dusk and off at your preferred time—whether it's midnight, early morning, or anywhere in between.
    • Season-Long Maintenance – If a bulb goes out or something shifts, we'll fix it—no worries, no hassle.
    • Takedown & Storage Options – At season's end, we carefully remove and pack your lights. Prefer us to store them? We offer secure off-season storage for added convenience.

    The result? A breathtaking holiday display without lifting a finger. Just sit back, sip some cocoa, and enjoy the magic.

    Our 4-Step Holiday Lighting Process
    • Step 1: Design & Customization - We start with a personalized consultation to craft a lighting design that matches your home, style, and holiday vision. From classic warm whites to bold and colorful displays, we bring your ideas to life.
    • Step 2: Expert Installation - Our team professionally installs your lights with precision—ensuring every strand is perfectly placed and securely fastened. No stress, no hassle—just a flawless display from day one.
    • Step 3: Enjoy the Glow - Set your preferred lighting schedule with easy, programmable timers—so your home shines when you want it to. Plus, if anything needs an adjustment during the season, we've got you covered with fast, hassle-free maintenance.
    • Step 4: Takedown & Storage Options - When the season ends, we carefully remove and pack up your lights. You can store them yourself or opt for our off-season storage service—so your lights are ready for next year without the extra clutter.

    Built to Shine – Quality & Warranty

    Our holiday lighting isn't just about festive flair—it's built for brilliance, durability, and reliability. We use commercial-grade materials designed to withstand the elements and shine brightly all season long.

    • Professional-Grade LED Lights – Energy-efficient, long-lasting, and vibrant for a stunning display.
    • Heavy-Duty Clips & Fasteners – Securely hold lights in place without damaging your home.
    • Weather-Resistant Design – Built to handle wind, rain, snow, and whatever the season brings.
    • 10-Year Warranty on Lights – Your holiday lights are covered for years of worry-free enjoyment.

    Looking for more than just lights? We also offer commercial-grade, pre-lit garlands, wreaths, and lawn décor to make your holiday showcase the best on the block.

    Lights Up. Stress Down.—Let's Go!

    Let's be real—stringing up lights is a holiday tradition no one actually enjoys. That's where we come in! iQ Home Care makes holiday lighting completely stress-free with professional design, expert installation, and hassle-free takedown.

    No tangled lights.
    No sketchy ladders.
    No late-night "why isn't this working" frustrations.

    Just a breathtaking display—designed, installed, and managed for you. Book your holiday lighting today and let's make your home the best-looking one on the block (without you lifting a finger).
