    A Clean Slate for Your Next Chapter

    Moving is stressful enough—cleaning shouldn't be part of the headache. Whether you're settling into a new home or ensuring your old one is spotless, iQ Home Care delivers a hassle-free, move-in/move-out clean that gives you a fresh start.

    We handle every detail—scrubbing baseboards, wiping down cabinets, and deep-cleaning appliances—so you can focus on the move itself. Our expert team ensures every inch is refreshed, polished, and ready for its next chapter.

    ✨ Leave nothing behind—except a perfectly clean home.

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Moving in? Moving out? Either way, you deserve a space that feels brand new. Our Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning goes beyond the basics, tackling every inch for a truly deep clean.

    • Every Room, Every Detail – Scrubbed, wiped, and polished top to bottom.
    • Cabinets & Drawers, Inside & Out – No dust, crumbs, or lingering messes left behind.
    • Appliances Deep-Cleaned – Fridge, freezer, oven, and stovetop—no grime, no problem.
    • Baseboards, Doorframes & Fixtures – The finishing touches that make a home feel fresh.
    • Tailored for Move-In & Move-Out Needs – Whether prepping for new occupants or making a clean start, we adjust to your needs.

    A truly clean home means a stress-free transition—let us handle the dirty work while you focus on what's next.

    Our 4-Step Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning Process
    • Step 1: Book & Customize - Schedule online in minutes or chat with our team to customize your service. Whether you need a pre-move refresh or a final clean-out, we tailor everything to your needs.
    • Step 2: Top-to-Bottom Deep Cleaning - We scrub, wipe, and polish every inch—inside cabinets, drawers, and appliances, plus baseboards, fixtures, and trim. No surface goes untouched.
    • Step 3: Final Touches & Inspection - Before we call it complete, we double-check every room, ensuring nothing is missed. Our standards? Move-in ready.
    • Step 4: A Fresh Start, Hassle-Free - Hand off the keys with confidence or step into your new home knowing it's spotless, polished, and ready for your next chapter.

    Built for the Big Move—Quality & Reliability You Can Trust

    FAQS

    Have Any Questions About Move-In/Out Cleaning?

    Our Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning is a comprehensive deep clean that includes scrubbing baseboards, wiping down cabinets inside and out, deep-cleaning appliances, and thorough cleaning of every room. We tackle every inch of your space to ensure it's spotless for the next chapter.

    We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance for move-in/move-out cleanings, especially during peak moving seasons. This ensures we can accommodate your preferred date and time. For move-out cleanings, schedule after all your belongings have been removed to allow our team complete access to all surfaces.

    You don't need to be present during the cleaning. As long as you provide entry instructions, our team will handle everything. For vacant properties, we can arrange key pickup and drop-off or use lockboxes for easy access.

    Move-in/move-out cleanings typically take 3-6 hours depending on the size of your home and its condition. For larger homes or those requiring extensive cleaning, we may recommend a team approach to complete the service efficiently in a single day.

    While we can't guarantee deposit returns (as landlords have varying standards), our move-out cleaning meets or exceeds standard cleaning requirements for most properties. We focus on the details that landlords and property managers typically check, and many of our customers report full deposit returns following our service.
