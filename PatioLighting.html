    Lighting That Transforms Your Outdoors

    The right outdoor lighting does more than just brighten the night—it enhances your home's architecture, highlights natural beauty, and sets the perfect mood for any occasion.

    Illuminate your landscape with soft, warm uplighting on trees and your home's architectural features. Create a cozy, inviting ambiance on patios with café lights, Edison bulbs, and festoon lighting. Increase safety and security with well-lit pathways, driveways, and entryways.

    At iQ Home Care, we design and install custom lighting solutions that bring your vision to life. Whether it's an elegant glow to frame your home's facade, a soft radiance over your backyard oasis, or statement lighting that turns your patio into the ultimate entertaining space, we make every outdoor moment shine.

    Elevate curb appeal. Extend outdoor enjoyment. Light up the night with style.

    Our Approach: What to Expect

    Outdoor lighting should be as functional as it is beautiful. At iQ Home Care, we create customized lighting solutions that complement your home's unique style while delivering the perfect balance of ambiance, security, and efficiency.

    What's Included?

    • Custom Lighting Design – We tailor each lighting plan to enhance your home's architecture, landscaping, and outdoor living areas.
    • High-Quality LED Fixtures – Energy-efficient, long-lasting, and built to withstand the elements.
    • Professional Installation – Clean, precise, and seamless—we secure and conceal wiring for a polished look.
    • Smart Controls & Timers – Easily schedule lights to turn on and off automatically for convenience and energy savings.
    • Weatherproof & Durable – Our commercial-grade fixtures are designed to last, even in harsh outdoor conditions.

    The result? A beautifully illuminated home that feels as good as it looks—enhancing your outdoor experience, night after night.

    Our 4-Step Lighting Installation Process
    • Step 1: Consultation & Design - We begin with a personalized consultation to understand your lighting goals. Whether you want warm architectural uplighting, elegant pathway lights, or festoon lighting for a cozy patio vibe, we'll design a lighting plan that fits your space perfectly.
    • Step 2: Professional Planning & Setup - Our team carefully maps out fixture placement to ensure optimal illumination. We take into account landscape features, facade highlights, and patio layouts for a balanced, cohesive look.
    • Step 3: Expert Installation - Using high-quality, weatherproof fixtures, we install your lighting with precision—hiding wires, securing mounts, and ensuring long-term durability. Whether it's stake lights, hardwired systems, or festoon installations, we handle every detail with care.
    • Step 4: System Check & Adjustments - Once installed, we test every fixture, adjust angles for the best effect, and ensure timers and smart controls are programmed to your preference. Your lighting is now ready to transform your outdoor space!

    Built for Beauty. Engineered to Last.

    Outdoor lighting isn't just about aesthetics—it's about lasting performance, energy efficiency, and year-round durability.

    • Premium-Grade Fixtures – Crafted from weather-resistant materials to endure rain, heat, and humidity without fading or rusting.
    • Energy-Efficient LED Technology – Low-energy, high-performance LEDs keep your home beautifully lit without spiking your power bill.
    • Smart & Dimmable Options – Adjust brightness, create ambiance, and set schedules for total lighting control.
    • Professional-Grade Wiring & Installation – Secure, low-voltage setups ensure long-term reliability with no messy cords or exposed wiring.
    • Warm White or RGB Options – While most homeowners love the classic elegance of soft white lighting, we also offer RGB color options for those who want extra versatility.
    • Industry-Leading Warranty – Backed by a comprehensive manufacturer's warranty for years of worry-free brilliance.

    Whether it's an elegant glow on your facade, a warm patio ambiance, or well-lit pathways for safety, our lighting is built to perform beautifully night after night.

    Set the Mood. Light Up the Night.

    Your outdoor space should be as inviting after dark as it is during the day. Whether you're highlighting your home's architecture, creating a cozy patio retreat, or adding security with well-placed lighting, we've got you covered.

    Let's bring your vision to life with a custom lighting design that enhances beauty, ambiance, and functionality—all in one seamless installation.
